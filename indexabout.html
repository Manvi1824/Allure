    ABOUT ME

    I love to work as a hair and makeup artist in weddings, be it at a romantic French Chateau or a chic city elopement. I enjoy to get to know my clients and each job is very important to me. I consider a really special honour to be there for your most special day!

    Here’s a few brief things I love

    I LOVE CREATING...

    Luminous, flawless skin

    Sultry eyes. And lashes, I adore lashes!

    I just happened to 'wake up like this but better' hair & a little undone chignons

    I FALL FOR...

    Champagne & dark chocolate, together and separately

    Beautiful handwriting and expensive stationery

    Little known perfumes - I want to create my own!

    I SPEND TOO MUCH ON...

    Skincare and trying out new lines

    Makeup brushes, I might have hundreds

    Beautiful houseplants that die due to all the travel

    I love working as a hair and makeup artist & stylist. I work mostly with destination weddings and elopements but I also work on ad shoots, TV and editorial photoshoots. I am based out of Sweden and during the wedding season also Paris. I work all around the UK, France, Italy, Sweden, Denmark and Europe wide.

    Through my job I meet the most incredible people and visit gorgeous locations. I love connecting with new people and no two weddings are the same. I have over 10 years of experience in high end weddings and I still get excited for each one.

    I would describe my style of makeup as chic, modern and always - always - what suits my clients the best. We work together to find the perfect look for your shoot, wedding or event.

    I also have a YouTube channel that started out as portfolio piece over a decade ago, and now has taken on a life of it's own. I've made friends, connections and met wonderful clients through it. I no longer update it but the memories ar all still live.

    I'm looking forward to meeting you, and if the time is right, get in touch with me here!
