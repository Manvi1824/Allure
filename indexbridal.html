    Whether your special day is an intimate elopement or a 3 day chateau wedding, I’m so excited for you and cannot wait to help you get ready!

    YOUR BRIDAL TRIAL

    Your bridal trial is an opportunity to discuss everything from how to match the style of your dress, colour scheme and the look you wish to create.

    We discuss your ideas and what you love. You can show me looks from Instagram or Pinterest you like and we'll adapt it to work for you on your big day. I’m also happy to guide you on how to choose the perfect makeup look and hair style for your big day. A bridal trial usually lasts 2-3 hours, at the end we will have established “the look” for your big day. We'll document your look with photos and video. While trials are usually only for the bride, trials can be organised for other members of the bridal party.

    Often with elopement weddings it can be difficult to have a trial - please don’t worry at all, this is totally normal for me! We can organise a FaceTime call so we can get to know each other and discuss your look.

    YOUR WEDDING DAY

    It's no secret the big can day can be stressful for the bride. And that’s why I aim create a relaxed environment and help you enjoy the time getting ready. I consider it an honour to a small part of your special day and I work hard to create a chilled out and relaxed atmosphere.

    I always turn up at your home or wedding venue in good time - I pride myself in being very reliable. I set up my workstation usually by natural light as that’s the most flattering and often most of your couple photos will be outside as well.

    I use high-end products so with all that smiling - and happy tears - you can rest assured that your makeup will stay perfect throughout the day. Your wedding photographs will last a lifetime, so it's important that you look your very best and feel beautiful. All you have to do is sit back and relax!!

    Before I leave, I'll leave you with a little touch up kit and tips on how to keep you looking radiant throughout the day into the evening.
